Transferring a photograph from a registered photographer

In 4 steps

  1. Find a registered photographer near you. Use our handy look-up tool and plan your visit. (When you log out from this website your application will be saved. What is a registered photographer?
  2. Get a unique code. Give this code to your chosen photographer when you visit.
  3. Visit the photographer. Your photo will be taken to passport standard and uploaded to the Official Photo Repository by the photographer.
  4. Log back in to this website. Your photo will then be added automatically to your application.
  5. Complete the application. Submit it online.
