Disabled parking bay

Standard procedures

  • Confirmation of your orange/blue badge status.
  • A visit by an engineer to assess the situation.
  • Consultation with your immediate neighbours.
  • Consultation with your Town/Parish Council.

Reasons for refusal

  • If adequate off-street parking facilities are available.
  • If there is an existing residents parking scheme.
  • Any other waiting restrictions in place.

To Register for a disabled parking permit you must prove your identity using an identity profile.

Register for an identity profile

on the GOV.UK website

Or sign in using your existing identity profile

Please note an ‘advisory disabled person’s parking bay’ is unofficial and non-enforceable and all successful applicants must notify us when the parking Bay is no longer required.

Disabled parking bay was last updated on July 2, 2013.
